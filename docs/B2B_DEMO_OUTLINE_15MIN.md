# Nestopia B2B Platform — 15-Min Demo Outline

**Audience:** US Partner  
**Focus:** B2B platform only (colleague covers B2C/consumer site)  
**Demo Site:** ai-nestopia.com  

---

## PART 1 — Opening (1 min)

> "You just saw the consumer experience. Now let me show you **the partner/dealer side** — where the real business happens."

**One-liner pitch:**

> We give small business owners and dealers **5 AI Agents** that handle the hardest parts of their job — **design, pricing, compliance, customer service, and knowledge management** — all in one platform.

---

## PART 2 — Live Demo (8-9 min)

### 1. AI Designer — *"The Deal Closer"* (3 min)

- Log in → go straight to **AI Designer**
- Upload a **customer's backyard photo**
- Select product type (Sunroom / Pergola / Zip Blinds)
- **AI generates a realistic rendering in ~30 seconds**

> **KEY LINE:** *"Instead of asking a customer to* ***imagine*** *a $50K sunroom, we* ***show them*** *exactly what it looks like in their backyard — in 30 seconds. This is the deal closer."*

**Prep:** Have a backyard photo ready. Have a fallback screenshot in case of network delay.

---

### 2. B2B Chatbot — *"One Conversation, Five Agents"* (2 min)

- Show the **floating chat bubble** (bottom-right corner)
- Type: *"How much for a 400 sqft sunroom?"* → routed to **Pricing Agent** (green badge)
- Type: *"What permits do I need in Maryland?"* → routed to **Compliance Manager** (blue badge)
- Expand to **full-page mode** → show conversation history & session list

> **KEY LINE:** *"Dealers don't need to learn 5 tools. They ask* ***one question in plain English***, *and the system* ***routes it to the right AI agent automatically.****"*

---

### 3. Company Operations + Projects (2 min)

- **Dashboard overview** — revenue, active projects, satisfaction score
- **Projects → Risk Heat Map** — color-coded risk at a glance (red/yellow/green)
- **Issues Tracker** — priority, status, assignee, due date

> **KEY LINE:** *"Project tracking, risk management, team performance —* ***one platform, zero spreadsheets.****"*

---

### 4. Knowledge Base Builder — *"The Secret Sauce"* (2 min)

- Show **47 documents indexed**, 6 categories
- Show **Agent routing tags** — each doc feeds specific AI agents
- Mention upload support: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, images, video

> **KEY LINE:** *"Dealers upload their own data — product specs, local building codes, pricing sheets — and* ***every AI agent gets smarter with their own data.*** *The more they use it, the harder it is to leave."*

---

## PART 3 — Closing (1 min)

> **"Three things no competitor can match at the same time:"**
>
> 1. **China supply chain** — 30-50% cost advantage over US-only competitors
> 2. **US local operations** — engineering center + regional dealer network
> 3. **AI platform** — 5 agents that get smarter with every interaction
>
> *"US players can't match our cost. Chinese exporters can't match our service. Neither can match our AI."*

Open for Q&A.

## Q&A Cheat Sheet

| Likely Question | Suggested Answer |
|----------------|-----------------|
| **How much does this cost dealers?** | Free during partner pilot; SaaS pricing after PMF validation |
| **How accurate is the AI design?** | Photo-realistic rendering — AI matches lighting, shadows, and scale to the real scene |
| **Can it handle my own product catalog?** | Yes — Knowledge Base Builder accepts custom product data and feeds it to all agents |
| **Data privacy?** | Multi-tenant isolation with row-level security — each dealer's data is **completely separate** |
| **What regions does Compliance cover?** | US-wide building codes, HOA rules, and permit requirements |
