## 四种户外产品特点与卖点 / Four Outdoor Product Lines — Features & Selling Points

---

### 一、共性 / I. Common Strengths (Across All Products)

> **量身定制**：针对客户需求，用AI赋能设计，24小时内提供最佳定制场景解决方案
> **Custom-Tailored Design**: AI-powered design delivers the best customized scene solutions within 24 hours, based on each client's specific needs

> **专业集采**：精选源头工厂，集中采购确保产品高性价比
> **Expert Sourcing**: Expert-selected source factories with centralized procurement ensure the best value for money

> **本地运营**：设有美国工程与技术服务中心和各地经销网络，提供售后服务
> **Local Operations**: US-based engineering & technical service center with regional dealer networks providing after-sales support

> **品质保障**：头部品牌企业生产，品质过硬
> **Quality Assurance**: Manufactured by leading brand-name factories with proven quality standards

> **AI 测量**：精准高效、节省人力
> **AI Measurement**: Precise, efficient, and labor-saving site measurement powered by AI

### 二、阳光房 / II. Retractable Sunroom

> **Classic Series**：抗雪载抗风压；抗冲击耐老化；高透光视野佳；模块化设计、安装节省人力
> **Classic Series**: Snow-load & wind-pressure resistant; impact-resistant & aging-proof; high light transmission with excellent visibility; modular design saves installation labor

> **Smart Series**：智能便捷（一键电动遥控）
> **Smart Series**: Smart & convenient — one-touch motorized remote control

> **Pro Series**：光伏发电储能（绿色节能）
> **Pro Series**: Solar power generation & energy storage (green & energy-efficient)

> **配置选型**：蜂窝遮阳隔热（避免高温炎热，保持室内舒适温度）
> **Optional Configurations**: Honeycomb shade system for thermal insulation (prevents overheating, maintains comfortable indoor temperature)

---

### 三、凉亭 / III. Pergola

> 电动翻转顶棚（灵活遮阳与采光）
> **Motorized Rotating Roof Canopy**: Flexible shading and natural light adjustment

> 多种透光面料卷帘（视觉效果佳）
> **Multiple Translucent Fabric Roller Blinds**: Excellent visual aesthetics

> 抗风雨能力强（结构稳固）
> **Strong Wind & Rain Resistance**: Structurally robust and stable

> 精心设计雨水收集系统（个人不建议强调和凸显雨水收集这个点，毕竟这是一款户外产品，规避客诉风险）
> **Engineered Rainwater Collection System**: *(Note from CG: Not recommended to highlight this point — as an outdoor product, it's better to avoid potential customer complaint risks)*

> **配置选型**：配风雨传感器，手机智能集成控制，LED灯光系统
> **Optional Configurations**: Wind & rain sensors, smartphone-integrated smart control, LED lighting system
